Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Our bot is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize the data we store to only what is strictly necessary for the bot to function:

  • Server Configuration: We store Discord IDs for your server, log channels, and immune roles/channels to apply your custom settings.
  • Statistics: We store numerical counters of interventions to provide the /stats command.

2. Real-time Processing

To protect your server, the bot processes message content and image attachments in real-time. This data is volatile:

  • Message hashes used for Anti-Spam are stored in temporary memory (RAM) and are cleared regularly.
  • Image attachments are downloaded temporarily to compute a SHA256 signature and are not saved on our disks.
  • Your content is never sold or shared with any third party. It is exclusively used locally by the bot's logic.

3. Third Parties

The only third party involved is Discord Inc., as the bot operates on their platform.
