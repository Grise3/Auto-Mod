Protect your community from hacked accounts

A discord bot specially designed to detect and stop hacked discord accounts. Whether sending images or sending invitation links for a server, It will detect and stop them.

Add to Discord Online Config

Behavioral Analysis

Monitors users for identical messages sent in a short timeframe. It identifies the "copy-paste" patterns typical of compromised accounts and wipes the spam.

Invite Links Interception

Scans for Discord invitations paired with specific suspect keywords. Can also be set to block every invite link entirely.

Image Similarity Detection

Uses advanced perceptual hashing (pHash) and SSIM to detect malicious images even if resized, compressed, or slightly modified. Reference images are pre-loaded in memory for instant detection.

Stateless Seeds

Encodes your entire protection logic, priorities, and thresholds into a single 15-character passkey for deployment or easy backups.
